Tacklebox

Tacklebox is a serverless open-source framework for providing webhooks as a service. Tacklebox enables you to provide webhooks to your users across multiple applications. Rather than requiring users to poll your API to get updates for events they care about, Tacklebox notifies them when those events occur.
